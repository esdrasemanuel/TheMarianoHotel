ABOUT US

The best of the Emerald Isle starts right here!

With a focus on Irish hospitality, quality accommodations, and a commitment to providing a personalized and memorable stay, The Mariano Hotel is dedicated to ensuring every guest experiences the unique charm of Dublin with comfort and ease. Whether you're exploring the historic landmarks, enjoying the vibrant local scene, or seeking a quiet retreat after a long journey, we strive to make your visit seamless and truly special.

Experience the invigorating pulse of Dublin - the laughter, the history, the music. Then, when the day is done, retreat effortlessly to our most exquisite and luxurious sanctuary. Your unforgettable Irish story starts the moment you receive our keys.
